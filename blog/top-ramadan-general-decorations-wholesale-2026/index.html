Sourcing General Decorations — Strategy for Wholesale Buyers

General decorations offer exceptional product-type diversity within a single supplier relationship — stickers, window films, honeycomb centrepieces, wall art, calendars, banners, and premium 3D boxes all ship from the same factory floor. This consolidation advantage means buyers can build a multi-category Ramadan assortment without managing multiple supplier relationships, quality standards, and shipping timelines. Carton efficiency varies by product type but remains strong across the category: stickers and window films achieve thousands of units per carton, honeycomb sets ship flat and expand at retail, and even the largest 3D decoration boxes pack efficiently due to optimized carton dimensions.

Our recommended three-tier strategy for building a general decorations assortment: start with 2–3 volume anchors at the $0.17–0.45 price range (stickers, window stickers) to fill impulse-buy display space and meet minimum order thresholds. Layer in 3–4 mid-tier products at $0.99–2.57 (honeycomb centrepieces, wall art, countdown calendars, wall banners) that command higher retail price points and anchor the visual display across multiple product formats. Top off with 1 premium category differentiator ($4.98 premium EID decoration box) that elevates the perceived quality of the entire assortment and reaches buyers that impulse-price SKUs cannot. Buyers who stock all three tiers consistently generate 40–60% higher category revenue than single-tier buyers, and the product-type diversity means retail customers encounter multiple price points and formats on every store visit.

Market-specific notes: Gulf region buyers favor gold-heavy designs across all product types — the Gold Islamic Wall Art (642194) and Premium Gold EID Box (642090) are the highest-reorder SKUs for Middle East accounts. European and North American buyers lean toward interactive and functional products — the Ramadan Countdown Calendar (613081) and Islamic Wall Hanging Banner (642179) outperform in these markets. Stocking both decorative and interactive formats ensures full geographic coverage. MOQ starts at one carton per SKU for stock designs. Custom sticker designs, calendar colorways, or banner greetings are available with a 600-unit MOQ per design.
