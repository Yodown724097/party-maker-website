Ordering Tips for LED Lights

LED lights are lightweight but vary significantly in carton density. Entry-level items like the LED Candle (641185) pack over 700 units per carton — ideal for container consolidation and low per-unit shipping. Larger decorative pieces like the Rainbow LED (641260) and USB Garland (641339) take more CBM per unit. Our recommended strategy: anchor your container with high-density volume items and top off with 2-3 premium SKUs for margin.

Battery notes: most LED products ship without batteries included, which simplifies customs clearance and avoids hazmat classification. Inform your end customers clearly — our product descriptions and packaging explicitly state battery requirements. For markets where batteries-included is preferred (some European retail chains), contact us about custom packaging options.
