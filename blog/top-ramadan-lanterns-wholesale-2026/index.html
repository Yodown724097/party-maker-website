7 Best-Selling Ramadan Lanterns for Wholesale Buyers in 2026

2026-06-08 · Product Spotlight · Party Maker

Why Lanterns Are the Heart of Ramadan Decor

Lanterns — or fanoos — are the most iconic symbol of Ramadan and Eid celebrations. For wholesale buyers, they consistently rank among the top-selling categories year after year. A well-curated lantern selection can anchor your entire Ramadan product line and drive repeat purchases from event planners, mosque suppliers, and retail chains.

We analyzed our 2026 sales data and customer feedback to identify the 7 lantern designs that wholesale buyers are reordering most frequently. Each listing includes the SKU, unit wholesale price, and what makes it a strong seller.

1. Bronze Moroccan-Style Lantern — SKU 641158 ($2.28/unit)

The bronze Moroccan lantern is the top seller in our ornament category. Its distressed metal finish gives an authentic, handcrafted look that premium retailers love. At $2.28 per unit, it delivers excellent perceived value — retail markup typically reaches $7.99-9.99. Available in 4 color variants (bronze, silver, black, gold), this series alone can fill a retail display.

2. Silver & Black Finishes — SKU 641159 ($2.28) & 641160 ($2.28)

The silver and black variants of the Moroccan lantern sell especially well in modern and minimalist home decor markets. European buyers prefer the silver for its elegant, understated look, while Middle Eastern buyers gravitate toward black and gold combinations. Stocking all four colors covers the full market spectrum.

3. White & Gold Mini Lanterns — SKU 641161 ($0.69) & 641162 ($0.69)

These compact lanterns at $0.69/unit are the volume play. Their low price point makes them perfect for party favor bags, table scatter decorations, and budget-friendly retail displays. At this price, retailers can sell them at $2.99 with a 77% margin or bundle 3 for $7.99. The white and gold are the two most ordered colors.

4. LED Candle Lantern 9x18cm — SKU 641267 ($1.88)

This LED-lit lantern combines the traditional fanoos shape with modern illumination. At $1.88 wholesale, it hits the sweet spot between affordable and premium. The built-in LED creates ambient lighting that photographs beautifully — critical for online retailers selling on Amazon, Etsy, or Instagram. Packed 12 per carton for efficient shipping.

5. Hanging Lantern Decoration — SKU 614110 ($0.34)

At just $0.34 per piece, this is the highest-margin lantern in our catalog. These lightweight hanging decorations work as ceiling displays, window accents, or tree ornaments. Because they're compact, you can pack over 1,000 units per carton — per-unit shipping cost is negligible. A $0.34 item that retails at $1.99 gives you an 83% margin.

Ordering & Logistics Tips

Lanterns are mostly hollow metal or lightweight plastic, which means they cube out before weighing out in shipping containers. Optimize your order by mixing heavy lanterns (SKU 641158-641160) with lightweight hanging decorations (SKU 614110) to balance container weight. MOQ starts at one carton for stock colors — custom colors or packaging require 600 pcs minimum per design. Most buyers order 2-3 color variants to test their market before scaling up.
