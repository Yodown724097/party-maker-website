Sourcing Table Decorations — Tips for Wholesale Buyers

Table decorations offer exceptional carton efficiency — most designs are flat-packed or compact, maximizing units per CBM and minimizing per-unit freight cost. A standard 20ft container can accommodate thousands of table decoration units alongside bulkier categories like lanterns and LED garlands. Our recommended strategy: build your table décor assortment across three price tiers — entry-level centrepieces ($0.46-0.58) for volume and impulse sales, mid-tier decorative pieces ($1.61-2.68) for core assortment, and one premium hero SKU ($6.71) for margin and category perception.

For markets where both Ramadan and Eid are celebrated, always stock both greeting variants. Our order data consistently shows that dual-greeting buyers achieve 30-40% higher category revenue than single-greeting buyers. MOQ starts at one carton per SKU for stock designs. Custom colours, greetings, or language variants are available with a 600-unit MOQ per design.
